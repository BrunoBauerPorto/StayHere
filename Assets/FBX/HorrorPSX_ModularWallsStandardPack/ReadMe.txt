Thank you for downloading HorrorPSX_ModularWalls. This modular kit is designed for retro horror-themed environments, offering flexibility and ease of use in your game projects.
Contents

    Models: .obj and .mtl files for all wall segments, doors, and assets.

    Textures: Includes BaseColor maps (1024x1024 for walls/structures, 128x128 for window glass).

How to use

    Import: Import the .obj files into your game engine (Unity, Unreal Engine, Godot, etc.) or 3D software.

    Materials: Create a new material and assign the BaseColor texture to your material's main color/albedo slot.

    Doors: Doors are separate objects. The pivot point is already placed on the hinge, making it easy for you to animate them to open/close.

    Walls:

        Interior Walls: Feature wall texture and baseboards on both sides.

        Perimeter Walls: Feature wall texture on the interior side and brick texture on the exterior side.
